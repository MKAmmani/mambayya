Director's Welcome

Mambayya House stands as a living monument to the democratic ideals that Mallam Aminu Kano championed throughout his distinguished life. We are committed to nurturing a new generation of democratic leaders, policymakers, and scholars who will carry forward the torch of good governance, civic responsibility, and social justice. Through our research, training, and archival programmes, we continue to build a repository of knowledge that serves Nigeria and the broader African continent.

Our library, museum, research centre, and e-learning platforms represent our dedication to making democratic knowledge accessible to all — from students and academics to government officials and civil society leaders.

Prof. Habu Mohammed.
Executive Director, Mambayya House – Aminu Kano Centre for Democratic Studies.

ABOUT MAMBAYYA HOUSE

A Centre Built on
Democratic Heritage

Named after the residence of Mallam Aminu Kano, a towering figure in Nigeria's democratic history, Mambayya House serves as a centre for research, dialogue, and civic education. Established to honor his legacy, the centre promotes democratic values through rigorous scholarship, public discourse, and innovative learning programmes that bridge the gap between academic research and practical governance.

biotech

Policy Research

Producing rigorous academic and policy research on democracy, governance, and public policy in Nigeria and Africa. Our research informs constitutional development, electoral reforms, and federalism studies that shape decision-making at all levels of government.

menu_book

Knowledge Preservation

Archiving Nigeria's democratic history through our museum, digital archive, and library collections. We preserve crucial documents, artifacts, and oral histories that tell the story of Nigeria's journey toward democratic governance and civic participation.

handshake

Capacity Building

Training civic leaders, government officials, and civil society through structured programmes and e-learning. Our comprehensive training initiatives empower stakeholders with the knowledge and skills necessary to strengthen democratic institutions and processes.
